Welcome Crystal collectors!

You are given random number at the start of the each round of the game.

There are four crystals below, each crystal represents a number. By clicking each crystal, you are adding up the points to your score.

The goal is to match your score with the given number. If your score is higher than the random number, you lose.

Not only does the target number change each round, but the value of each crystal will change too.

Good Luck!
